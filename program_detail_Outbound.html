Program Overview

Our Singapore Inbound Student Study Tours offer an unparalleled opportunity for students to immerse themselves in Chinese language and culture while experiencing Singapore's unique multicultural environment. This comprehensive program combines structured classroom learning with authentic cultural experiences, creating a transformative educational journey.

Students will engage with native speakers, explore historical sites, participate in traditional activities, and develop practical language skills through real-world interactions. The program is designed to accommodate various proficiency levels, from beginners to intermediate learners.

Program Highlights

  • Daily Chinese language classes with certified instructors
  • Cultural immersion activities and workshops
  • Visits to iconic Singapore landmarks and cultural sites
  • Interactive sessions with local students and families

Target Audience

  • High school and university students
  • Chinese language learners (HSK 1-3 level)
  • Cultural enthusiasts and exchange students
  • Educational institutions and study groups
